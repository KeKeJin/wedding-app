  • The Airport
    John Wayne Airport (SNA)
    Southern California has many airports but John Wayne - Orange County Airport (SNA) is the easiest for domestic flights and is about 20 miles from The Richard Nixon Library.

    Here are some additional airports in the area:
    • Ontario International Airport/ONT: 30 miles North from the Venue.
    • Long Beach Airport/LGB: 30 miles West from the Venue.
    • Los Angeles International Airport/LAX: 45 miles West from the Venue (traffic might not be the best)
  • The Hotel
    Embassy Suites by Hilton Brea North Orange County
    The Embassy Suites Hotel in Brea is about 5 miles from The Richard Nixon Library. The Link offers guests a discount off the best available flexible rate. The rate will fluctuate based upon pricing of the hotel at the given time.

    Here are some additional hotels in the area:
    • Fullerton Marriott at California State University
    • Fairfield Inn by Marriott Anaheim Hills Orange County
    • Best Western Plus Anaheim Inn
